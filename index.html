We are conducting practical workshops and seminars for the teachers and college lecturers on different topics to motivate them and also variety of programs to propagate the essence ancient Indian culture with special emphasis on peaceful coexistence and values.

VIVEKAYUGA Foundation has also successfully organized number of programs in which free note books have been distributed to the needy students. Scholarships and free uniforms have been given and blood donation camps have been organized on different national days.

Jeevana Shikshana Abhiyana

Personality of a human being gets a halo when the knowledge acquired is based on the foundation of morality. It is in this context that the foundation has organized a number of programs under the banner of “Personality Development - Jeevana Shikshana Abhiyana”. This has benefited already more than 50,000 students.

Yuvaprerana

Today’s urgent need is to find appropriate ideals in various walks of life and place them before students whom they can emulate. Such workshops twice or thrice each year have attracted 300 to 500 youth every time. We have a keen desire to expand the horizon of this program.

Shikshaka Prerana Samavesh

Education is a flow and not a stagnant pool of water. It has to be renewed and invigorated with new thoughts and ideas. Teachers meet is such a special program where teachers interact with experts in the field to get new insights. This program has received tremendous response from the teaching community.

Vivekayuga Prakashana

In order to perpetuate our culture, it is essential that we should document and pass it on to the next generation, the noble ideas of our culture, art, and civilization.

In this direction, VIVEKAUGA Publication has already brought out 3 books – ‘JEEVANA SHIKSHANA’, ‘PREETIYE JEEVANA’ for students and “NAANUNDRE NANGISTA” for all. We publish a yearly volume entitled ‘VIVEKAYUGA’ every year. This is a humble effort to help youngsters who are eager to transform their potentialities into realities.

We are happy to share with you that, we have organized Seminars in association with Karnataka Tourism Department “YOUTH AND TOURISM” and “SHIKSHAKA PRERANA” a teacher’s motivation program for BBMP Teachers and also we have conducted “HUMAN RIGHTS AND THE ROLE OF WOMEN” - a seminar for college students at Maharani Lakshmi Ammanni College for Women, Malleshwaram. And also conducted “HUMAN RIGHTS AND THE ROLE OF YOUTH” a seminar for college students at Sheshadripuram Degree College, Yelahanka, “POWER OF VOTE” an awareness program for college students at Sheshadripuram Main College, Bangalore... “YUVAPRERANA Program” at SSMRV Degree College at Jayanagar, Bangalore... and many more.
